This tool is designed specifically for students to easily grasp the fundamental concepts of how computer hardware works together. 

## 🎯 What will you learn?
By using this application, you will understand:
1. **Hardware & I/O:** The different parts of a computer and how data travels between them via the Bus System.
2. **CPU & The Machine Cycle:** How the "brain" of the computer actually thinks and processes instructions step-by-step.
3. **Memory & Storage:** The difference between RAM, ROM, and your hard drive, and why computers calculate storage sizes differently than humans do.

## 🚀 How to use this app for learning

1. **Explore the Tabs:** At the top of the page, you will see four tabs. Start from left to right. Read through the explanations, analogies, and examples provided in each section.
2. **Read the "Teacher Guide Boxes":** Look out for the yellow boxes with a lightbulb or question mark icon! These are designed to answer common questions and provide fun analogies (like comparing a CPU to a chef) to help you connect the dots before moving to the next topic.
3. **Switch Languages:** If you prefer to learn in Chinese, simply click the "中文" button at the top right corner. You can switch back and forth at any time!
4. **Test Your Knowledge:** Once you have read through the first three tabs, click on the **"Knowledge Check"** tab. 
   - Answer the multiple-choice questions based on what you've learned.
   - Click "Submit Answers".
   - **Crucial Step:** Whether you got the answer right or wrong, *read the detailed explanation box* that appears below each question. This is where the real learning happens!
